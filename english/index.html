Varde commune celebrates 577 years

In 1442, King Christoffer confirmed Varde City's merchant rights, it is now 577 years ago. The city is older, though the year 1442 marks in many ways an important gap in the history of the city.

The jubilee will of course be marked. The celebration takes place in 3. August - 6. August with the "Medievel Ages" as the theme. The Medievel Ages has inspired a host of exciting experiences and activities for the whole family.

On Thursday and Friday the celebration takes place in Varde city center, where the theme is freely interpreted by the municipality's cultural institutions and the city's associations. The authentic Medieval atmosphere you can experience on Saturday and Sunday among knights and medieval people on the market square at Varde river.

We hope that you will join us and help us make the celebration both festive and joyful.
